About

Hello. I'm Tyler - an aspiring front end developer and recent graduate of The Iron Yard. This is a website. Whether you're here because I know you, you want to check out my work, you're stalking me on social media, or any other reason, I won't judge you.

A little about my background, I'm from Jacksonville, FL, and I love it here. I graduated from UCF with a degree in Finance. But after 4 years working in the financial sector, I ultimately decided to pursue something completely different. I now get excited and dream about JavaScript, among everything else I'm learning as a front end developer. I'm still pretty green and have a lot to learn, but the prospect of continuous learning and the chance to create fuels my passion for development.

If you're interested in my work, I've included some of my projects in the portfolio section and you can download my resume. Also, it ceases to be stalking if we actually connect on social media. Reach out. Links are below.

Good Neighbor

I created this web app as my final project at The Iron Yard. This app aims to connect you with your neighbors by matching you with others in your neigborhood where you can interact and build relationships.

  • Tech Stack:
  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • MaterializeCSS
  • ReactJS
  • jQuery
  • Firebase
  • Google Maps API
  • Zillow Neighborhoods API
  • Eventful API
